CM Aviation, a leader in aviation education, consultancy, and helicopter rentals, aimed to establish a robust online presence showcasing its diverse offerings. My focus in this collaboration was on creating an innovative website that seamlessly integrated information about courses, consultancy services, and helicopter rentals.

> Explore the live website at [cmaviation.it](https://cmaviation.it)

### Project Overview

Guided by my commitment to minimalism and clarity, I crafted a visually compelling and user-friendly website. The objective was to create an online platform that not only communicated the breadth of CM Aviation's offerings but also provided an engaging experience for prospective students and clients seeking consultancy services.
